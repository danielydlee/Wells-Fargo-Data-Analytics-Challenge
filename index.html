Introduction to Wells Fargo Data Analytics Challenge

Dialogues on social media nowadays can provide tremendous insight into the behaviors, desires, pains, and thoughts of consumers. This project that our group worked on developed a repeatable process that identifies, classifies, and extracts the underlying drivers of consumer financial conversations and comments in social media data.

Question #1: What financial topics* do consumers discuss on social media and what caused the consumers to post about this topic?
